The Importance of Typography in Web Design

By Aksa Mary John • January 2026 • 5 min read

Typography is more than just choosing attractive fonts. It plays a critical role in how users read, understand, and engage with content on the web.

Why Typography Matters

Good typography establishes visual hierarchy, improves readability, and guides users through the content effortlessly. Poor typography, on the other hand, can confuse readers and increase bounce rates.

Typography and User Experience

Font size, line spacing, and contrast directly impact accessibility. Well-structured text allows users to scan articles quickly and find the information they need.
